Social Media Etiquette Checklist for College Students 

The Do's
Do use privacy settings to control who can see your personal posts and information.
Do engage in professional networking by following industry leaders and alumni on LinkedIn.
Do double-check facts and sources before sharing news or information to avoid spreading misinformation.
Do use clear, respectful, and professional language when commenting on public forums.
Do showcase your technical projects and certifications to build a positive digital footprint.

The Don'ts
Don't post confidential information about your college, professors, or peers without permission.
Don't engage in "flame wars" or aggressive public arguments with organizations or individuals.
Don't share inappropriate photos or content that could be viewed negatively by future recruiters.
Don't over-tag posts with excessive or irrelevant hashtags that make content look like spam.
Don't use informal slang or "text-speak" when communicating on professional platforms like LinkedIn.
